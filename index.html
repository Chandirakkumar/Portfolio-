Case Study

From Classroom to
Clickable Simulation.

Client

Karnataka Bank

Scope

AML / Compliance Training

Tools Used

Storyline 360, After Effects, QuickLearn LMS

Duration

6 Weeks

Outcome

92% completion rate, 40% reduced L&D cost

01

The Problem

Karnataka Bank's AML training was entirely classroom-based — low engagement, inconsistent delivery, and no way to track learner progress. Compliance risk was high as staff struggled to recall procedure under real scenarios.

02

The Solution

Designed a branching simulation in Articulate Storyline 360 with realistic banking scenarios — where learners made decisions as a frontline officer. Integrated motion-graphic intros via After Effects and published to QuickLearn LMS with xAPI tracking.

03

The Result

The module achieved a 92% course completion rate in its first deployment, with post-assessment scores averaging 84%. The bank reduced its annual L&D travel costs by an estimated 40% by shifting to self-paced delivery.

Anti-Money Laundering Simulation

A multi-branch scenario simulation for Karnataka Bank's AML compliance mandate. Learners play the role of a frontline bank officer navigating suspicious transaction flags, customer interactions, and escalation decisions.

Built using Storyline 360's advanced branching and custom JavaScript triggers, the simulation featured five scenario paths, animated characters, and integrated knowledge checks — published via xAPI to QuickLearn LMS for tracking.
